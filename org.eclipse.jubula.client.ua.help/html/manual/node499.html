6.3.1 ITE

The ITE is the interface between you and the AUT . It provides you with browsers to see Test Cases and Test Suites , and editors to modify them.

In the client, you specify your tests without any programming. You can write tests modularly, structure them to fit your needs, and easily change them to follow developments in the AUT .
